Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2025

1. No Personal Data Collection

We do not require login or registration. You remain anonymous—we don’t collect personal data like name, email, or contact info.

2. Anonymous Usage Data & Advertising

To support the free version, we display ads via Appodeal, Meta Audience Network, and Google AdMob. We collect only anonymous usage data (e.g. app crashes, session duration, advertising ID, IP) to analyze performance and improve stability. We do not link this data to your identity.

3. Subscription and Billing

Subscriptions (weekly, monthly, yearly) are processed via Google Play and Apple App Store. We do not collect or store payment methods, transaction records, or billing data—these are managed entirely by Google/Apple.

4. Purposes & Legal Basis

  • Anonymous usage data: collected to improve app quality—legal basis: legitimate interest.
  • Subscription access: facilitated by Google/Apple—legal basis: performance of a contract.

5. Third-Party Data Sharing

No personal data is shared. Aggregated data may be processed by advertising networks and analytics providers. Subscription handling is exclusively performed by Google and Apple.

6. Data Retention

  • Anonymous usage data: stored in aggregate form indefinitely for performance tracking and analysis.
  • Billing/subscription data: retained by Google and Apple per their policies; we do not store any of it.

7. User Rights

Because we don’t collect personal-identifiable data, rights to access, modification, deletion, or portability don’t directly apply. You can manage ad preferences or revoke consent via your device settings or by canceling subscriptions in Google Play or the App Store. Mixpanel allows end-users to opt out of tracking per GDPR compliance.

8. Security

We apply standard technical safeguards to protect anonymous data. Mixpanel secures data in transit (TLS) and at rest; supports EU data residency to comply with GDPR.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The updated effective date will be listed; continued use implies acceptance.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy questions, please email apps.con.ai@gmail.com.
